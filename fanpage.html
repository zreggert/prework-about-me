About the Artist

John Sturgill Simpson is a singer-songwriter from Jackson, Kentucky whose music is strongly influenced by well known outlaw country artist such as Merle Haggard, Willie Nelson, and Waylon Jennings. However, while he is known mostly as a country artist, Simpson's albums range widely from traditional country to rock and psychedelic and most recently bluegrass.
